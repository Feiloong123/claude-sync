Always accompany research content (materials synthesis, catalysis mechanisms, DFT methods, etc.) with specific literature citations that include DOIs.

**Why:** User does academic research and needs traceable, verifiable references for every claim. Without DOIs, references are not directly usable.

**How to apply:** After any research-oriented answer, provide a reference list with: authors, year, journal abbreviation, title keywords, and DOI. Conference proceedings without DOIs should be noted as such. For web-search results, look up DOIs proactively rather than just citing URLs.
